Contemporaneous notes on grounds, observations, and decisions protect the public and the officer. Record why a stop started, how long it lasted, and how it ended.

When Gordon II or any tool provides guidance, verify critical legal points against current statute, policy, and supervisor direction before acting.

This system is decision support — the officer remains accountable for choices in the field.
