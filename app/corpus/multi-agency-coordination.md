title: Multi-agency resource picture — simulated vs live dispatch
category: lookout
source: Operational coordination training summary, not legal advice
tags: backup, fire, paramedic, coordination, simulated, dispatch, eta
---

Officers may use a resource picture (backup, fire, EMS) to plan containment and co-response — but only live authorized dispatch systems provide operational orders.

Training demos may show **simulated** unit locations and ETAs to practice sequencing; those markers are not live AVL and must not be treated as real dispatch.

Use distance, cover, and containment when backup is far. An ETA is context for pacing, not a command to delay necessary lawful action or to rush without grounds.

Document requests for backup, fire, or paramedics contemporaneously with the reason and scene conditions.
