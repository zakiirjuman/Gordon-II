Pursuits and emergency responses must weigh public safety against apprehension. Active road restrictions and construction hubs may eliminate safe pursuit routes — consider containment and air support per policy instead of entering closed ramps or work zones without exigency.

Exigent circumstances are narrow: imminent harm, destruction of evidence where delay defeats the purpose, or hot pursuit tied to a serious offence — not convenience.

Coordinate with traffic operations when diverting around major closures.
