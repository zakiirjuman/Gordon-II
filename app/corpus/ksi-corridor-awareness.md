KSI (killed or seriously injured) collision history for a street or ward indicates elevated harm potential — useful for traffic enforcement priorities, school-zone vigilance, and resource placement.

Use KSI data for **place-based** risk awareness, not to infer suspicion about individuals or demographics passing through the area.

Pair collision hotspots with live restrictions and construction when briefing patrol resources.
