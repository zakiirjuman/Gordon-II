If backup is not close, avoid compressing time unless there is imminent harm. Use distance, cover, lighting, and containment to keep options open.

When a person is agitated near traffic, construction, or a closure, the environment can raise risk for everyone. Position first, communicate second, and close distance only when the safety need is clear.

A backup ETA is operational context, not a command to wait. Officers remain accountable for lawful and proportionate decisions in the moment.
