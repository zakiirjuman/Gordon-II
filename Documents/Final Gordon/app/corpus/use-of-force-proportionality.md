title: Use of force — proportionality and de-escalation (educational summary)
category: authority
source: Common police accountability principles / Criminal Code ss. 25–27 context — training summary
tags: force, proportionality, de-escalation, safety, subject
---

Force must be necessary, proportionate to the threat, and cease when resistance ends. Prefer communication, distance, and time when safe.

Higher-risk environments (construction zones, expressway ramps, KSI corridors) increase hazard to officers, subjects, and bystanders — factor environmental risk into tactical choices without using environment as pretext for unrelated enforcement.

Report and document use of force beyond routine handcuffing according to service policy.
