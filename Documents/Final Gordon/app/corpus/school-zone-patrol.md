category: lookout

---

Active schools near your post increase pedestrian, cyclist, and parent drop-off traffic — especially at arrival and dismissal windows.

Treat nearby school locations as a lookout factor, not a reason to stop vehicles without independent lawful grounds under the Highway Traffic Act.

When enforcing near schools, position for sight lines, reduce speed, and avoid blocking crosswalks or bus loading zones.

Document why the stop location was chosen and how school-zone context affected officer safety positioning.
