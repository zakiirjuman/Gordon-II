---
id: do-not-pretext-stops
title: Do not — pretextual or fishing-expedition stops
category: do_not
source: Charter / appellate principles — training summary, not legal advice
tags: pretext, fishing, stop, charter, overreach
---

Do not use a minor traffic infraction as a pretext to investigate unrelated criminal activity without independent reasonable grounds.

Do not extend a traffic stop beyond the time needed to address the stop purpose unless new grounds arise — document any extension.

Do not treat a construction zone or collision hotspot as automatic reasonable suspicion for every occupant.
