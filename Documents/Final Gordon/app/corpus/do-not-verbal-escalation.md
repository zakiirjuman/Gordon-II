---
id: do-not-verbal-escalation
title: Do not — verbal escalation under stress
category: do_not
source: Procedural justice and de-escalation training summary, not legal advice
tags: de-escalation, verbal, stress, dignity, commands, overreach
---

Do not use humiliation, sarcasm, or unnecessary threats to gain compliance. These tactics can make a tense contact more dangerous and harder to justify afterward.

Do not stack multiple conflicting commands when a person appears confused, intoxicated, injured, or in crisis. Give one clear instruction at a time when safe.

Do not let an environmental hazard, crowd pressure, or delayed backup become a shortcut around lawful grounds, proportionality, or documentation.
