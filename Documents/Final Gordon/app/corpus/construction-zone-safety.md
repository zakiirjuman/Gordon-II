source: Toronto Transportation Services / RODARS — operational context

Active lane closures and construction hubs reduce sight lines, narrow lanes, and increase worker and cyclist presence.

Expect unpredictable merges near restriction endpoints. Position emergency vehicles to avoid blocking remaining through lanes when possible.

Cross-reference restriction descriptions with collision history on the same corridor before staging enforcement.
