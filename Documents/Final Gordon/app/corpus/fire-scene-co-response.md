title: Fire scene co-response — coordination and scene safety
category: lookout
source: Multi-agency response training summary, not legal advice
tags: fire, co-response, scene safety, apparatus, coordination, lookout
---

When fire operations are active or a fire hall is nearby, expect apparatus access, hose lines, and command posts to control parts of the roadway.

Do not obstruct fire routes or staging areas. Coordinate with the fire incident commander before changing perimeter positions.

Police roles may include traffic control, crowd management, and evidence preservation — each requires a lawful basis and clear handoff.

Treat fire-station proximity as infrastructure context for backup and co-response planning, not as automatic grounds for unrelated enforcement.
