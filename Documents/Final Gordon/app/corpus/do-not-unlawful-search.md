---
id: do-not-unlawful-search
title: Do not — search beyond lawful scope
category: do_not
source: Charter s. 8 — training summary, not legal advice
tags: search, warrant, consent, charter, scope
---

Do not search a vehicle, phone, or bag without warrant, valid consent, or a recognized common-law/statutory exception.

Do not rely on vague “officer safety” to justify exploratory searches unrelated to a specific safety concern.

If consent is sought, it must be voluntary and informed; note who consented and to what scope.
