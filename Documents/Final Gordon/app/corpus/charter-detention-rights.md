Officers must be able to articulate lawful authority for any detention or arrest. A detention is a suspension of liberty by state authority; it must be brief and based on reasonable suspicion connected to a recent or ongoing offence, not a hunch.

Upon arrest or detention where investigation continues, inform the person of the reason and their right to counsel without delay where required. Searches incident to lawful arrest are limited by scope and purpose; fishing expeditions are not authorized.

Document grounds contemporaneously: what you observed, when, where, and why it justified the level of interference chosen.
